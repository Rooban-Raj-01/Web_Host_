About Us

Etherion is the premier technical club of SRM TRP Engineering College focused on Networks, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing. We aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions.

Our Mission

To create a community of tech enthusiasts who can innovate and secure the digital future.

Achievements

Won multiple national-level cybersecurity competitions and successfully implemented campus-wide network solutions.

Community

150+ active members collaborating on projects and research papers in emerging technologies.
